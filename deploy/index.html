What is MSAT?

Manufacturing Science and Technology (MSAT) is a specialized function within pharmaceutical companies that bridges R&D and commercial manufacturing. MSAT teams ensure process robustness, lead technology transfers, resolve production challenges, oversee validation, and maintain GMP compliance. They “think like industrial engineers who consider industrial-scale production and everything that goes into building the perfect factory.”

Why it’s booming: Unprecedented pharma CAPEX ($475B US commitments, $300B+ global greenfield/brownfield), biologics expansion, GLP-1 drug demand (Ozempic/Wegovy/Mounjaro), cell & gene therapy scale-up, mRNA platform diversification, and biosimilar market growth.

User Guide

What is this CRM?

This is Alessio's personal Career CRM — a tool built specifically to manage your job search in the pharma MSAT / Technology Transfer space. It stores your target contacts, tracks outreach status, provides tailored communication guides based on each person's DISC profile, and keeps all your career research in one place.

Everything is saved in your browser's local storage. Export regularly to keep backups.

CRM View — Contacts Table

The main view shows all your contacts organized by DISC personality group. Each row displays:

  • # — Priority ranking (1 = highest priority target)
  • Name / Title — Contact name, job title, and any badges (Danger Zone, Follow-up reminders)
  • Company — Links to the company's careers page
  • Email — Click to open your email client
  • DISC — Personality type code (see DISC section below)
  • Remote — Work arrangement: Yes (remote), Hybrid, or On-site
  • LinkedIn — Direct link to their profile
  • Status — Pipeline stage dropdown

Expanding a card reveals the communication guide (how to write to this person based on their personality), an editable tailored message, notes area, last-contacted date, and full contact details.

Filters let you narrow by DISC type, role category (MSAT, Ops, Talent Acquisition), or active-only status. The search bar filters by name, company, or title.

DISC Personality System

Each contact has an estimated DISC type that guides how you should communicate with them:

D — Captain (Dominant)

Direct, results-oriented, decisive. Communication: Be brief, lead with outcomes, skip small talk. They respect confidence and efficiency.

I — Motivator (Influential)

Enthusiastic, collaborative, optimistic. Communication: Be warm and energetic, show personality, emphasize people and vision.

S — Supporter (Steady)

Patient, reliable, team-oriented. Communication: Be sincere and supportive, don't rush, show you value relationships and stability.

C — Analyst (Conscientious)

Analytical, precise, quality-focused. Communication: Be detailed and factual, provide data, avoid vague claims. They value accuracy.

Two-letter codes (e.g. "CD") indicate a primary type with a secondary influence. "CD" = primarily Analyst with Captain traits.

How Framework Contracts Work

1
EU (DG INTPA) issues call for tenders divided into thematic “Lots”
2
Large consortia (NIRAS, Particip, Ecorys, etc.) bid and win Lot-specific framework contracts
3
When a project arises (e.g. “Support clean production in Tunisia”), EU sends Request for Services to the contractor
4
The framework contractor searches their expert database for matching profiles
5
YOU register with these consortium firms to be in their expert databases. When selected, you sign a short-term subcontract (daily rate + per diem + travel).
